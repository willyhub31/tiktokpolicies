Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Le8Geek processes information when users access the web app, authorize TikTok Login Kit, and upload videos through the TikTok Content Posting API workflow.

Information we process

  • Basic TikTok account data returned after user authorization.
  • OAuth callback information needed to complete sign-in and account linking.
  • Video files, captions, posting status, and workflow metadata selected by the user.
  • Operational logs required for troubleshooting, security, and upload monitoring.

How information is used

  • Authenticate the user and connect the authorized TikTok account.
  • Prepare, review, and upload selected videos as drafts or private posts.
  • Maintain service reliability, prevent abuse, and investigate technical issues.

Data sharing

Le8Geek does not sell personal data. Information is shared only with service providers and platforms required to operate the workflow, including TikTok APIs and storage or hosting services used by the app owner.

Data retention

Data is retained only as long as needed for workflow operation, review history, security monitoring, legal obligations, or user support.

User choices

Users can revoke TikTok access through TikTok connected-app settings and can request data access, correction, or deletion by contacting Le8Geek support.
